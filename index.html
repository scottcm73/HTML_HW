Summary: Latitude vr. X

The purpose of this project was to examine the relationship between latitude coordinates and weather. The latitude coordinates were randomized, and then an API selected the city closest to the coordinates. In this way the dataset includes latitude and longitude coordinates around the world. The nearest city is the point where the measurements were taken. The coordinates were compared to the several different measurements and plotted on graphs with the measurement on the y axis. These graphs are visible under the plots pulldown menu in the top right.

Matplotlib was used to plot the scatter plots of latitude versus the various measurements. This site offers the original source data and features visualizations as part of the annalysis. Additionally, descriptions of any observed trends and correlations witnessed were described.
